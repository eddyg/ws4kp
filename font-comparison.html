What to look for: The CM (Consistent Metrics) versions should have identical baseline positioning across all browsers and operating systems, while the original fonts may show different positioning depending on your platform.

Technical details: The original Star4000 fonts have conflicting font metrics in different tables (HHead, OS/2 Win, OS/2 Typo), causing browsers to position text differently. The CM versions have unified metrics for consistent cross-platform rendering.
